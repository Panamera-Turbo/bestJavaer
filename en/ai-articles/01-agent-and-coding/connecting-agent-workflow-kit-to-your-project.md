So how does it get into your project?

The roughest way: feed the entire Chinese document in the warehouse to AI and ask it, "What kind of workflow should I use?"

More importantly, use it to wrap it up.

```text
Evaluate this repository using $agent-workflow-kit-zh-cn and recommend the appropriate AI workflow level.
```
