# MTC mode tried

And then I tried the MTC model to help me plan a slightly more complex project -- a simple post management backstage.

MTC mode does look more like Claude Code's plan Mode. It analyses your needs, lists technical options, catalogue structure, database design and then generates a detailed implementation plan. It didn't start until you confirmed it.

This model is for a scenario where you haven't figured out what to do and need an AI to help you figure it out. Like you want to do something, but not sure what technology is, MTC will give you a few options for your choice.

But to be honest, MTC is a little too cautious for someone like me who already knows what I want. I prefer Code's "dry first."
