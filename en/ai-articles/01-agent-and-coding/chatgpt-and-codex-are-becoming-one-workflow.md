Codex didn't disappear.

More precisely, the Codex capability would enter the ChatGPT and the independent Codex entrance would still exist. The developers will still use it in App, CLI, Ide, GitHub.

The rights were not automatically released.

OpenAI Help Center writes clearly that plugins in Business, Enterprise, Edu workspace will follow workspace apps.

The administrator can disable the app from the working-space settings and control who can access through RBAC.

ChatGPT and Codex are not all mixed up.

The official help document mentions that the two sides are still separated, but some settings and connected services may be followed.

For example, you picked up Google Drive in ChatGPT, and it could be used in Codex; you could be disconnected at any time.

Agent really wants to get into the workflow, not as easy as demo, but as the authority, audit, context, confirmation mechanism.
