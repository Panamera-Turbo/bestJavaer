Yesterday Alibaba announced a complete ban on Claude Code.

Since July 10, internal employees have been completely prohibited from using this tool in the office environment, and the self-developed Qoder has been recommended as an alternative.

I think everyone should know the reason for this ban. It is because Claude Code was exposed to the security risk of implanting backdoors and accurately locked 147 Chinese domain names. Mainstream domestic technology companies and AI laboratories such as Meituan, NetEase, Baidu, Alibaba, and ByteDance are all on the monitoring list.
