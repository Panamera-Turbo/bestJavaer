# And the true contrast with the "Traditional Program"

Speaking of which, we all have to ask the question: and deploy a lobster/other AI Agent system better than this?

I've helped an entrepreneurial team with similar jobs. It took about two weeks for the server to be selected, for the environment to be configured and for long-term maintenance. This is not difficult for a person with a technical background; but for him, the two weeks are cost, hesitation, and the reason for not doing it first.

The core value of the **popx workshop is this: to compress the time from ideas to sharing links from two weeks + to ten seconds.**

| |Popx Workshops|Traditional deployment programme|
|---|---|---|
|From thought to online.|Ten seconds.|1 day - 2 weeks|
|Technical background required|No need.|Yes.|
|Server costs|Free.|Monthly average of 200+|
|Cellular Management|Native support|Need to work with other tools|
|Customise Space|Limited|Infinity|

** One judgement**: this tool is not replacing technological capabilities, but skipping the demand for technological capabilities. If your business/project needs to be highly customized, sooner or later you have to return to the code. But if your claim is "quick verification of an idea" "quick access to a viable prototype", the efficiency of this thing is crushing.
