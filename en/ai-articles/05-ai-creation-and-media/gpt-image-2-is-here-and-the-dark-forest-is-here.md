# The problem of not keeping up with tests

It was said that the ability to generate has grown and the ability to detect will follow. This is a dynamic game, but the generation capacity is currently ahead of it.

Technical tests have several lines of thought: looking at metadata, analysing statistical characteristics at pixel level, and trace model fingerprints. But each has its limitations. Metadata can be deleted or falsified, pixel-level features become less visible after they are produced and trace fingerprints are difficult to maintain after models have been iterative.

Even more problematic is the fact that, in practice, images are transcribed, compressed, relayed, and edited twice. Every step, the remaining AI features are a little less, and by the time the images reach you, they may not be detectable.

So the reality is that the generation end is running, the test end is chasing, and it's pushing hard.**
